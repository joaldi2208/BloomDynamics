1. Preliminaries
Jonas opened the meeting and summarised the objectives of the meeting: define the aim of the project and get access to data. 
 
2. Reports
Bastien and Estel introduced the project: the project is a collaboration with 'Voice of the ocean' who deploy several gliders in the Baltic. Glider data used in our project will refer to 2-3 gliders in the period of March 3 to September. The different gliders are equally calibrated and have the same setting of sensors (no systematic variations expected). Gliders move continuously a few centimeters per second from surface to a max. depth of 80m. One glider profile corresponds to ~0.5km.
